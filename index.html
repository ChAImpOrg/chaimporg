Result: a complete website rebuild delivered in under 24 hours at zero cost to the organization — live and serving the rescue community immediately.

About the Founder

British by birth.
American by choice.
Mission-driven.

After 25+ years at Microsoft — across Engineering, Product, and Support — James Whelan founded ChAImp to do something that hits different.

British by birth and American by choice, the same rigor, reliability, and pragmatism he applied across the full Microsoft ecosystem now goes to work for the nonprofits that need it most. Organizations that know technology could transform their impact — but don't have the time, skills, or budget to get there.

ChAImp is his give-back. For many of the organizations he works with, the engagement is entirely pro bono. Built with whatever technology serves the mission best — because the outcome always matters more than the stack.

The name ChAImp — pronounced "champ" — comes from somewhere personal — a reminder that championing others is the most important thing any of us can do.

ChAImp SaaS

Software for needs the market forgot — or priced out.

Most nonprofit software is either too expensive, too generic, or built for someone else entirely. ChAImp SaaS is different. Three products — CartonCrew, SchoolPTA, and RescueCentral — each co-built with a partner organization to solve a real problem the market underserves. Every one is designed around the same belief: AI should enhance the admin experience, not replace it — so your team has more time on mission.

How It Works

Real need. One partner.
Shared solution.

Our SaaS isn't built in a vacuum. Each product begins as a specific problem inside a specific nonprofit — and grows into something the wider sector can use.

01

Surface a real gap

A partner nonprofit tells us about a need their current tools don't meet — or that commercial software prices well beyond their budget. We listen first.

02

Co-build with the partner

We design and build alongside the people who'll actually use it. The first organization gets a solution shaped to their workflow — not a generic platform.

03

Open it to the sector

Once proven, the product becomes available to other nonprofits facing the same gap — at pricing that respects mission-driven budgets, including free tiers where we can.

Our SaaS Principles

Built for the sector,
not extracted from it.

Commercial nonprofit software too often treats the sector as a market to mine. ChAImp SaaS operates by a different set of rules.

Nonprofit-appropriate pricing

Pricing scales with what an organization can realistically pay — including free tiers where the mission justifies it. No enterprise pricing dressed up with a discount badge.

Co-developed, not assumed

Every product is shaped with at least one real partner nonprofit before it goes broader. Features come from observed need — not from a roadmap meeting.

Your data, your call

You own your data. You can export it. We don't train commercial models on it. Governance is a default, not an upsell.

No lock-in by design

Open formats, documented exports, and a graceful exit path. If a different tool fits you better later, we'll help you move — not hold your data hostage.

Hosted with care, or self-host

Use our managed hosting, or run it on your own infrastructure. Either way, the product behaves the same.

ChAImp retains the IP

So the product can keep serving the sector long after any single engagement ends. Co-build partners get preferential terms, not invoice surprises.
